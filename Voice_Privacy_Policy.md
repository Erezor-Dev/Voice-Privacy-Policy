# Privacy Policy – Voice

*Last updated: April 2026*

This privacy policy describes how the Voice application 
("the app", "we") collects, uses, and protects information 
in the context of its use.

## 1. Data Controller

Data is processed in the context of using the Voice 
application. For any questions regarding this policy or 
your data, you can contact us via the contact details 
provided on the app's store listing.

## 2. Data Collected and Purposes

### 2.1 Account Data
When creating an account, the app collects your email 
address to identify you and synchronize your data across 
your devices. Authentication can also be performed via 
Google Sign-In, in which case the data transmitted is 
subject to Google's privacy policy.

### 2.2 Voice Data and Notes
The app records audio that you dictate in order to 
transcribe it into text. These recordings are transmitted 
to a third-party transcription service and are not 
retained after processing. The transcribed content 
(notes, projects) is stored locally on your device and, 
if you have created an account, synchronized to our 
servers to enable multi-device access.

### 2.3 Usage Data
The app retains data related to your use of the 
transcription service (minutes consumed per month) in 
order to manage the quotas associated with your 
subscription.

### 2.4 Technical Data
Technical information (device type, operating system) 
may be used to ensure the proper functioning and 
compatibility of the application.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services to provide its 
features. These partners may process certain data in 
accordance with their own privacy policies.

**OpenAI (Whisper)**
Used for audio transcription. Recordings are sent to 
OpenAI solely for transcription purposes and are not 
retained after processing.
Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

**Anthropic (Claude)**
Used for automatic organization, titling, and 
summarization of notes. Note content may be transmitted 
to Anthropic for these processing operations.
Policy: https://anthropic.com/privacy

**Supabase**
Used for data storage and synchronization across 
devices. Your notes and projects are hosted on 
Supabase servers.
Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

**RevenueCat**
Used for in-app subscription management. RevenueCat 
may collect data related to your purchases.
Policy: https://revenuecat.com/privacy

**Google**
Used for authentication via Google Sign-In.
Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on:
- The performance of the service agreement (provision 
  of app features)
- Your consent for optional features
- Our legitimate interests in improving the service

## 5. Data Retention

- Audio recordings are deleted immediately after 
  transcription
- Notes and projects are retained for as long as your 
  account is active
- Upon account deletion, your data is retained for 
  30 days and then permanently deleted, unless you 
  sign back in within that period

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), 
you may have rights such as: access to your data, 
rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, 
objection, and portability.

Account deletion is available directly in the app: 
**Profile → Delete my account**.

For data processed by third parties, you may exercise 
your rights with those partners via the links provided 
in section 3.

## 7. Security

Your data is protected by:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest on our servers
- An access control system ensuring that each user 
  only accesses their own data

## 8. Children

The app is not intended for children under the age of 
13. We do not knowingly collect personal data 
concerning them.

## 9. Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of the last update 
is indicated at the top of this document. An up-to-date 
version will be accessible via the URL provided on the 
app's store listing.

## 10. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or your 
personal data, please use the contact details provided 
on the app's store listing (App Store / Play Store) or 
within the app.
