# Account Deletion – Voice

To delete your Voice account and all associated data:

1. Open the Voice app on your device
2. Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner
3. Scroll to the bottom of the profile screen
4. Tap "Delete my account"
5. Confirm by typing "SUPPRIMER" in the dialog

## What happens after deletion

- Your account will be soft-deleted immediately
- All your notes, projects, and data will be inaccessible
- After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted from our servers
- If you sign in again within 30 days using the same email, 
  your account and data will be restored automatically

## Can't access the app?

If you cannot access the app to delete your account, contact us 
via the support email listed on the app's store page and we will 
delete your account manually within 7 days.
